how much?

  • 4-6 chicken pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • vegetable oil
